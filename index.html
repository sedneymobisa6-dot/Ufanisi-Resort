Our Story

A Jewel in the
Hills of Kisii

Located atop one of the many surrounding hills dotting Kisii County, Ufanisi Resorts is a jewel nestled within five acres of lushly landscaped terrain — complete with a natural tropical rainforest. The resort beckons guests to experience quiet peace, reconnect with nature, and wake up to the unforgettable sound of birdsong.

From guided hikes up the famed Manga Hills, to arranged tours of the Maasai Mara — every day at Ufanisi is curated around extraordinary experiences. Truly, "a good place to be."
