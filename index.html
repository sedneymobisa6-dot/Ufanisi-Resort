Our Story

A Good Place
to Simply Be

Located atop one of the many surrounding hills dotting Kisii County is Ufanisi Resorts — a jewel nestled within five acres of lushly landscaped terrain complete with a natural tropical rainforest to boot.

The resort beckons and entices guests to partake in the quiet peace, reconnection to nature and the unforgettable sound of chirping birds — reminiscent of a truly eco-friendly environment. You will never want to leave.

Whether you come for business, celebration, honeymoon, or simply to rest — Ufanisi delivers the kind of hospitality that stays with you long after you depart.

Activities & Adventures

Local Experiences

From Manga Hills to the Maasai Mara — Ufanisi connects you to the best of Kisii and beyond.

01
⛰️
Manga Hills Hiking

Guided hiking trips up the famous Manga Hills — one of the most spectacular walks in Kisii County with breathtaking panoramic views of the highlands.

02
🦁
Maasai Mara Safari

Arranged tours to the world-famous wildebeest migration — the Eighth Wonder of the World — at the Maasai Mara through the Oloolunga Gate.

03
🗿
Tabaka Soapstone

Visit the world-famous soapstone carvers at Tabaka — a uniquely Kisii cultural experience found nowhere else on earth. Beautiful, handcrafted art.

04
🌿
Forest Walks & Birdwatching

Explore five acres of tropical rainforest on the resort grounds — home to hundreds of bird species and rare indigenous plants unique to the Kisii highlands.

05
🏊
Pool & Leisure

Unwind by our pristine swimming pool with full poolside service throughout the day. The ideal way to do nothing — beautifully.
