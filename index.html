Our Story

A Good Place
to Simply Be

Located atop one of the many surrounding hills dotting Kisii County is Ufanisi Resorts — a jewel nestled within five acres of lushly landscaped terrain complete with a natural tropical rainforest.

The resort beckons guests to partake in the quiet peace, reconnection to nature and the unforgettable sound of chirping birds. It is a truly eco-friendly environment — one you will never want to leave.

Whether you come for business, celebration, honeymoon, or simply to rest — Ufanisi delivers the kind of hospitality that stays with you long after you depart.
